About

I am Wanxing Wu (吴宛幸) (CV), an M.S. student in Computer Science at Institut Polytechnique de Paris (IP Paris), with a B.S. in Data Science and Big Data Technology from Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), currently a Research Assistant at SUSTech-NLP (advised by Prof. Guanhua Chen).

My research is on how heterogeneous-capability LLMs should work together. I started with per-query routing: in RouterXBench, I built a three-axis evaluation framework that decouples router intrinsic ability from scenario fit. The same composition question reappears when an agent has to adapt to a new environment over many steps; in Scope-then-Cover, I instantiate this as a single explore action where the strong actor scopes a small candidate subspace and frozen cheap workers verify it in parallel, effective in both test-time adaptation (TTA) and online experiential learning (OEL) settings.
