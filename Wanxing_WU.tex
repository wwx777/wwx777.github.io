\section{About}

I am an M.S.\ student in Computer Science at \textbf{Institut Polytechnique de Paris (IP Paris)}, with a B.S.\ in Data Science and Big Data Technology from \textbf{Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech)}, currently a Research Assistant at \textbf{SUSTech-NLP} (advised by Prof.~Guanhua Chen).

\vspace{3pt}
My research is on \textbf{how heterogeneous-capability LLMs should work together}. I started with per-query routing: in \textbf{RouterXBench}, I built a three-axis evaluation framework that decouples router intrinsic ability from scenario fit. The same composition question reappears when an agent has to adapt to a new environment over many steps; in \textbf{Scope-then-Cover}, I instantiate this as a single explore action where the strong actor scopes a small candidate subspace and frozen cheap workers verify it in parallel, effective in both test-time adaptation (TTA) and online experiential learning (OEL) settings.
